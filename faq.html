FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Edited on on July 13, 2020

Q: What do you write about?
A: Whatever I feel like writing about. In the past, it's been a lot about college classes and experiences, work thoughts, and life thoughts. Currently, I think it will be more about my journey to express myself artistically as well as life thoughts trying to navigate adulthood.

Q: Will you ever play in a professional table tennis tournament again outside of collegiates?
A: Probably not. Pingpong has been a big part of my life for so long, I'm now exploring different fields outside of it. Not that I've completely forgotten pingpong, but it's taken a backseat to other priorities.

Q: You have a lot of run-on sentences.
A: I try to write the way I talk, so sometimes it sounds good in my head when I write stuff, but it ends up being kind of crazy in writing. Also, I don't extensively edit my blog posts, so feel free to email me any grammatical errors or changes you see.
